# Customer Churn & Revenue Optimization

## Business Problem

A subscription-based company is experiencing customer churn that is impacting recurring revenue.
The objective is to identify drivers of churn, predict at-risk customers, and recommend actions to reduce churn while maximizing revenue.

## Stakeholders

- Business leadership
- Marketing team
- Customer success team

## Decision to Support

- Which customers are at risk of churning?
- What interventions are most cost-effective?
